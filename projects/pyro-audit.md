# Full-Scope Thermodynamic Heat Balance of a Cement Kiln Pyro-Processing Unit
**Attock Cement Limited** | *Line-2 Thermal Energy Optimization*

---

## 🔍 Project Scope
To perform a comprehensive thermal energy audit of the kiln system, I conducted a simultaneous heat and mass balance across the pyro-processing boundaries. The study was based on **1 kg of clinker** as the functional unit to standardize efficiency metrics.

<div style="background-color: #f8f9fa; border-left: 5px solid #003366; padding: 15px; margin: 20px 0;">
  <b>Instrumentation Utilized:</b> Pitot Tubes (Gas Velocity), Thermocouples (Temp), Pressure Gauges, and Portable Gas Analysers. All instruments were 3rd-party calibrated to ensure 99%+ data integrity.
</div>

## 📐 Thermodynamic Framework
The audit required calculating the enthalpy of every stream entering and leaving the system boundary.

### Energy Balance Equation
$$\sum Q_{in} = \sum Q_{out} + Q_{losses}$$

Where the Specific Heat Consumption ($SHC$) is primarily driven by the Heat of Combustion of fuel vs. the Heat of Formation of clinker.

## 📊 Audit Results (Line-2)
The following table summarizes the energy distribution calculated during the audit ($kcal/kg_{clinker}$):

| Heat Input Source | Value | Heat Output Source | Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fuel Combustion** | 783.37 | **Formation of Clinker** | 408.32 |
| **Sensible Heat (Raw Meal)** | 15.78 | **Pre-Heater Exhaust** | 224.50 |
| **Cooler Fans Air** | 6.14 | **Cooler Vent Losses** | 91.63 |
| **Primary/Blower Air** | 0.61 | **Radiation/Convection** | 52.38 |
| | | **Clinker Discharge** | 17.94 |
| **TOTAL INPUT** | **805.90** | **TOTAL OUTPUT** | **803.87** |

**Audit Variance:** 0.25% (Indicating extremely high measurement accuracy).

---

## 💡 Engineering Insights
* **Pre-Heater Efficiency:** Identified that exhaust gases carry ~28% of total heat input, suggesting potential for further waste heat recovery (WHR).
* **Loss Mitigation:** Quantified radiation and convection losses (52.38 kcal/kg), leading to a review of kiln refractory health.
* **Accuracy:** Successfully balanced the system within a negligible margin of error, providing a reliable baseline for future CapEx energy-saving projects.
