## 1. Project Overview
I supervised the **Cold Commissioning** and **Quality Assurance (QA)** testing for a significant system upgrade at Attock Cement. This CapEx project involved integrating new automation logic with existing mechanical hardware to improve throughput.

## 2. Technical Engineering Analysis
To validate that the new equipment met the vendor's design specifications, I performed independent verification of the fluid dynamics.

### Airflow & Pressure Drop Validation
I calculated the system's pressure drop ($\Delta P$) to ensure the fans could handle the resistance of the new ducting layout.

$$\Delta P = f \cdot \left( \frac{L}{D} \right) \cdot \left( \frac{\rho v^2}{2} \right)$$

* **Calculations Performed:** * Volumetric airflow rates vs. design requirements.
  * Friction factor analysis for new piping/ducting runs.
  * Motor load verification during dry runs.

## 3. Scope of Work
* **QA Testing:** Systematically tested 50+ I/O points for the PLC interface.
* **Cold Commissioning:** Verified motor rotations, valve actuators, and interlock logic without process material.
* **Documentation:** Translated complex technical data into **Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)** for the operations team.
