## 📐 Engineering Methodology
The project required balancing the grinding efficiency with the circulating load of the mill.

### Energy Audit & Mass Balance
I utilized "Before vs. After" audits to ensure the Specific Energy Consumption ($SEC$) remained stable despite the higher load:

$$SEC = \frac{P_{consumed} \text{ (kW)}}{\dot{m} \text{ (TPH)}}$$

Where:
* $P_{consumed}$ is the total power consumed by the mill motor.
* $\dot{m}$ is the mass flow rate of coal.

### Redesign Focus Areas
1. **Feed Optimization:** Adjusted the separator speed and feed rate to prevent mill "choking."
2. **Thermal Stability:** Monitored the heat exchange to ensure coal moisture remained <1% for optimal grinding.
3. **Air-to-Fuel Ratio:** Fine-tuned the primary air fan settings to transport the increased coal volume.
