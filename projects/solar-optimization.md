## 📐 Statistical & Engineering Methodology
The core of the project was using **Hypothesis Testing** (Z-tests/T-tests) to validate if the variance in generation intervals was statistically significant enough to trigger specific load-shedding or startup protocols.

### Predictive Load Modeling
We analyzed the generation efficiency ($\eta$) based on irradiance ($G$) and temperature ($T$):

$$P_{out} = A \cdot \eta \cdot G \cdot [1 - 0.005(T_{cell} - 25)]$$

### Variance Analysis
I performed an operational gap analysis to align shift scheduling with peak renewable generation. By analyzing the "Supply-vs-Demand" delta, I presented a business case to leadership to restructure operational hours, maximizing the use of free solar energy over expensive grid power.
